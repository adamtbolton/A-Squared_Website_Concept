Meet the Team:

Dr. Ann Connolly

Co-Founder & Senior Consultant

With over 20 years of experience in senior leadership roles at Johnson & Johnson, UCB, and GSK, and a PhD in Biochemistry, Ann is a highly accomplished executive with a strong track record of success in driving innovation and business development within the life sciences industry. Throughout Ann's career, they have been responsible for supporting and investing in early-stage innovations, managing global business development transactions, and overseeing preclinical development activities. With a passion for finding the best science and technology to solve unmet medical needs, Ann is committed to nurturing the global innovation ecosystem to ensure its continued vibrancy.

Adam Bolton

Co-founder & Consultant

As a former strategy consultant, Adam has a proven track record of delivering results for clients. He developed a go-to-market strategy for an early-stage biotech, by analysing customer needs and preferences, market segments, and competitive landscape to identify key drivers of adoption and growth. During his pursuit of an MSc in Economics and Finance at King's College London, he managed a student-led investment portfolio. Adam also holds a BSc in Biomedical Science from the University of Reading, which provided him with a solid foundation in cellular, molecular, and biochemical sciences, with a particular emphasis on the study of human anatomy and physiology, diseases, and their mechanisms.
